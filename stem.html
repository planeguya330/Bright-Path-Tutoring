Quick Start Guide: Run Your First Program

1
Locate the Controls: Find the code editor on the left side of the simulator screen and the virtual Arduino board on the right side.
2
Start the Simulation: Click the green "Play" button near the top left of the simulator window.
3
Observe the Result: Look at the micro-controller board on the right. You will see a small LED labeled 'L' turn on and off every second!
4
Mini Challenge: Stop the simulation (red button). In the code on the left, find the line that says delay(1000); and change it to delay(150);. Click Play again to make the LED blink like a strobe light!
